FAQ

Is this a funding company or a software tool?

BizCredit OS is a software operating system focused on structure, tracking, and guidance. It’s designed to support your funding strategy, not to replace lenders or underwriters.

Do you guarantee I’ll get business credit without a personal guarantee?

No. We don’t make funding guarantees. We help you build a stronger, more credible profile over time—so when you apply, you’re not guessing what lenders are looking at.

Can I use this if I’m just forming my LLC?

Yes. In fact, “day zero” entities are ideal. You can build correctly from the beginning instead of cleaning up later.

Will you tell me exactly which banks or vendors to use?

BizCredit OS can help you think in terms of relationships and reporting, but specific institutions, offers, and approvals will always depend on your location, industry, and risk profile.
